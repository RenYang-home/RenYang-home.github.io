Biography

I am a Senior Applied Scientist at Microsoft Copilot, working on research and development of Computer-Use AI Agents. Previously, I was a Senior Researcher at Microsoft Research on AI-powered future media experiences, and a Senior Algorithm Researcher at SenseTime. I obtained my Dr. sc. at the Computer Vision Lab, ETH Zürich, under the supervision of Prof. Luc Van Gool and Prof. Radu Timofte.
